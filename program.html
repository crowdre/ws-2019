The 3rd International Workshop on Crowd-Based Requirements Engineering (CrowdRE'19)



23 or 24 September 2019
Jeju Island, South Korea
Co-located with RE 2019

Program

CrowdRE 2019 is planned to be a full-day workshop held on either 23rd or 24th of September, 2019. CrowdRE will be co-located with the RE 2019 on Jeju Island, South Korea.

CrowdRE 2019 will include a keynote, paper presentations, and an interactive session. The workshop intends to promote lively discussions among participants from both industry and academia. After each paper presentation, a discussant puts the paper into a scientific perspective, lists the contributions, and raises issues and questions for discussions. At the end of each paper track, an open forum will stimulate a lively discussion on the papers, and the proposal of new ideas.

Our full program will be announced early August 2019.

Important Dates
  • Paper Submission: 5 July 2019

  • Camera Ready: 8 August 2019
